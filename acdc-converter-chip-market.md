<p><p>The AC/DC converter chip market is primarily categorized into linear AC/DC converters and switch-mode power supplies (SMPS). Linear converters provide simple, low-noise power conversion, often used in low-power applications where efficiency is less critical. In contrast, SMPS are more versatile, offering higher efficiency and compact designs, making them suitable for a wide range of applications, including consumer electronics and industrial equipment. Both types cater to different power requirements and design constraints in various sectors.</p></p>
