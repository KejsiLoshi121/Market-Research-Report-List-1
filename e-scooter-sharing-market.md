<p><p>The e-scooter sharing market is primarily divided into two types: dockless and station-based systems. Dockless e-scooter sharing allows users to pick up and drop off scooters anywhere within a designated area, providing flexibility and convenience. In contrast, station-based systems require users to pick up and return scooters at specific docking stations, offering more controlled locations and parking. Both models aim to enhance urban mobility, catering to different user preferences and operational challenges within shared transportation networks.</p></p>
