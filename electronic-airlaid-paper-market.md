<p><p>The electronic airlaid paper market is characterized by two primary bonding techniques: chemical bonding and thermal bonding. Chemical bonding involves the use of adhesives or binders to create strong bonds between fibers, enhancing the paper's durability and performance in various applications. In contrast, thermal bonding utilizes heat to fuse fibers together, providing a reliable and efficient bonding method that improves the paper's strength and texture. Both methods cater to diverse end-use industries, including hygiene products, packaging, and wipes.</p></p>
