<p><p>The Military Grade Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) market features three key types: Passive Standby, Online, and Line-Interactive. Passive Standby UPS systems offer basic protection by switching to battery during outages, providing minimal filtering. Online UPS systems deliver continuous power and advanced protection by converting incoming AC power to DC and back to AC, ensuring clean power even during fluctuations. Line-Interactive UPS units combine features of both, utilizing automatic voltage regulation to manage surges and maintain power quality without engaging the battery.</p></p>
