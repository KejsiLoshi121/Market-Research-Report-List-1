<p><strong>The Cell Phone Cellular Repeaters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog Signal Booster</li><li>Digital Signal Booster</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cell phone cellular repeaters enhance mobile signal reception by amplifying existing signals. The market primarily features two types: analog signal boosters and digital signal boosters. Analog signal boosters amplify incoming signals without distinguishing between different frequencies, making them suitable for basic applications. In contrast, digital signal boosters convert analog signals into digital formats, optimizing them for specific frequencies and providing superior performance in environments with complex signal conditions. Both types aim to improve connectivity and reduce dropped calls in areas with weak signals.</p></p>
