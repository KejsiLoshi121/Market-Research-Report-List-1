<p><p>The Non-Contact Laser Extensometers market comprises two primary types: Built-in Laser Extensometers and Frame Laser Extensometers. Built-in Laser Extensometers are integrated into testing systems or structures, providing real-time measurement of surface displacement without physical contact. In contrast, Frame Laser Extensometers are standalone units designed to monitor displacement on larger specimens or components. Both types leverage laser technology for precision and accuracy in measuring strains, especially in sensitive applications where traditional methods might interfere with the material being tested.</p></p>
