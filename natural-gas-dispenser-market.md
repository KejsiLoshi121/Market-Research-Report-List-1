<p><p>The Natural Gas Dispenser market can be categorized into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I refers to dispensers that deliver compressed natural gas (CNG) and are commonly used in fueling stations for vehicles. These systems typically operate at high pressures. Type II dispensers, on the other hand, provide liquefied natural gas (LNG) and are designed for applications requiring cryogenic temperatures. Type II dispensers are often utilized for larger vehicles and industrial applications, ensuring versatility in natural gas applications.</p></p>
