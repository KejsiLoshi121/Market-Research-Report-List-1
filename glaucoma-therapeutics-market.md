<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Glaucoma Therapeutics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prostaglandin Analogs</li><li>Beta Blockers</li><li>Alpha Agonists</li><li>Carbonic Anhydrase Inhibitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glaucoma therapeutics market includes several key drug classes. Prostaglandin analogs enhance aqueous outflow, making them first-line treatments. Beta blockers reduce aqueous humor production and are often used as adjuncts. Alpha agonists also decrease production while increasing outflow, offering additional management options. Carbonic anhydrase inhibitors further reduce fluid production and can be administered topically or systemically. Together, these therapeutic types help manage intraocular pressure, crucial for preventing vision loss in glaucoma patients.</p></p>
