<p><p>In the lens market, various polymer materials are utilized for their distinct properties. CR-39 is a lightweight and scratch-resistant plastic, ideal for prescription lenses. Polycarbonate (PC) offers high impact resistance and UV protection, making it suitable for safety glasses. Cyclo Olefin Copolymer (COC) is valued for its clarity and low moisture absorption, enhancing optical performance. Poly(Methyl Methacrylate) (PMMA, Acrylic) is known for excellent optical clarity and UV resistance. Polystyrene is economical and lightweight, often used for projector lenses or less demanding applications.</p></p>
