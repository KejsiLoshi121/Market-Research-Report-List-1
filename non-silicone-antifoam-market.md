<p><p>The Non-Silicone Antifoam market is categorized into water-based and oil-based types. Water-based antifoams are formulated to disperse easily in aqueous environments, making them ideal for applications in food processing, pharmaceuticals, and wastewater treatment. In contrast, oil-based antifoams are more effective in hydrophobic systems, such as industrial oils and lubricants, providing superior control over foam in high-temperature or high-viscosity conditions. Both types serve critical roles in enhancing process efficiency across various industries by minimizing foam formation.</p></p>
