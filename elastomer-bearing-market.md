<p><strong>The Elastomer Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Neoprene Bearing</li><li>Laminated Elastic Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elastomer bearing market primarily consists of neoprene bearings and laminated elastic bearings. Neoprene bearings are made from synthetic rubber and are known for their outstanding resistance to weather, oil, and aging, making them suitable for various structural applications. In contrast, laminated elastic bearings consist of layers of elastomer reinforced with steel plates, providing enhanced load distribution and damping capabilities, especially in seismic regions. Both types are essential for improving the flexibility and durability of structures.</p></p>
