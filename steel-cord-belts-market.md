<p><strong>The Steel Cord belts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Strand Steel Cord Conveyor Belts</li><li>Layer Steel Cord Conveyor Belts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel cord belts market primarily comprises two types: strand steel cord conveyor belts and layer steel cord conveyor belts. Strand steel cord belts consist of steel wires arranged in parallel to enhance tensile strength and durability, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, layer steel cord belts feature multiple layers of steel cords, providing additional resistance to impact and wear. Both types are widely used in industries such as mining, construction, and logistics, where reliable material handling is crucial.</p></p>
