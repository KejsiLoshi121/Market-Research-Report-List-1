<p><p>Shielded Type Tunnel Boring Machines (TBMs) encompass two primary market types: Single Shielded and Double Shielded. Single Shielded TBMs are designed for softer ground conditions, providing support to the tunnel face and allowing for excavation while shielding the operator from earth falls. In contrast, Double Shielded TBMs are engineered for more challenging geological conditions, offering enhanced protection and stability through both the front and rear sections, thus facilitating tunneling in mixed or difficult ground environments more effectively.</p></p>
