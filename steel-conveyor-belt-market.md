<p><p>The Steel Conveyor Belt market encompasses various types, primarily distinguished by construction and application. Strand Steel Cord Rubber Conveyor Belts feature closely spaced steel cords running longitudinally, providing high tensile strength and durability, suitable for heavy-duty operations. Layer Steel Cord Rubber Conveyor Belts, on the other hand, consist of multiple layers of steel cords, offering additional flexibility and resistance to impact and tear. Both types are essential in industries like mining and logistics for efficient material handling and transportation.</p></p>
