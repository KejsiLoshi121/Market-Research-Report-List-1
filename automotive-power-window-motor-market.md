<p><p>The automotive power window motor market features two main types: DC 12V and DC 24V motors. DC 12V motors are predominantly used in most vehicles due to their compatibility with standard automotive electrical systems, offering efficient operation and lower power consumption. Both types are essential for ensuring smooth and reliable window operation in various automotive applications.</p></p>
