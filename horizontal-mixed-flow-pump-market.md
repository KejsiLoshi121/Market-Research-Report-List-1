<p><strong>The Horizontal Mixed Flow Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-open Impeller</li><li>Open Impeller</li><li>Enclosed Impeller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The horizontal mixed flow pump market comprises three main types of impellers: semi-open, open, and enclosed. Semi-open impellers feature blades that are partially shrouded, offering a balance between efficiency and ease of handling solid materials. Open impellers have no shroud, providing excellent flow capabilities for fluids with larger particulate content. Enclosed impellers, fully surrounded by a casing, provide high efficiency and are suited for clean, low-viscosity fluids, preventing any leakage and ensuring reliable performance across various applications.</p></p>
